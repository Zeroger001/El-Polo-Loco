The chickens have had enough!

After decades of factory farming and genetically modified feed, the chickens developed the ability to think more complexly than any animal ever had before. They realized their current situation and started a revolution against humanity. But just as their march was about to begin, an inconspicuous Samaritan crossed their path. His love for chicken and extra spicy salsa would stop their advance!

Goal of the game:

Reach the end of the level and defeat the boss of the chicken army!

 Defeat enemies by jumping on their heads or throwing extra spicy salsa at them.
Collect salsa bottles to increase your ammunition.
Collect 10 coins to receive healing.

Good luck, lone hero!
